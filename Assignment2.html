CH Bait & Tackle

Mission Statement

The website that I am building is called "CH Bait & Tackle". This website is a representation of a dream of mine which is to make some type of product and make a website to advertise and sell my given product. I decided on fishing and fishing gear to be my product and my main focus because fishing is a huge hobby of mine that I know alot about, and it is how I spend most of my days in the summer. I want to inform and help others who want to fish, or need advice.

Three goals that I hope my website will achieve

There are three goals that I hope my website will achieve which include being:

  1. Effective: I want my website to be straight to the point. This means that all of my viewers can access the content/products that they are looking for with ease.
  2. Clean: I want my website to be visually appealing/clean. This means that all of the content is very pleasing towards my given audience in the sense that there is not too much crammed onto the page. I want my audience to enjoy and admire how my webpage looks and interacts.
  3. Likable: I want my website to be an enjoyable experience for all of users. This means overall have a website that allows for simplicity and satisfaction. I want my audience to feel impressed and satisfied while on my website.

Model Website

The website that I chose to model my website around is called: "Master Bait & Tackle". This site is a perfect representation of how I want my website to operate and look. This website is very organized, easy to operate, and visually satisfying. It contains most of the navigation websites that I want to implement onto my website. And lastly, the home page is filled with important information that shows its customers who they are and what they have to offer.

Content

For my final project, I will have a total of 5 individual web pages which include:
  1. Main Home Page: The Main Home Page will be the "hub" for all of the general information. (About, Products, Contact, Learn About Fishing, Photo Gallery)
  2. About Page: The About Page will contain information about my brand "CH Bait & Tackle" such as how it was established, about the author, and our dedication for the customers.
  3. Products Page: The Products Page will contain all of my fishing gear that I will create that can be purchased by customers.
  4. Learn About Fishing Page: The Learn About Fishing Page will contain information for beginners on the basics of fishing, and my tips and tricks.
  5. Photo Gallery Page: The Gallery Page will be a site where I can upload and share pictures from my personal fishing trips.

Target Audience

My target audience is anyone who has a general interest in fishing, and wants to learn more about it. On my webpage end-users will be able to shop for fishing gear, explore a fishing gallery, learn about fishing tips/tricks, and learn more about the author.
